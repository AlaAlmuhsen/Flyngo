About Flyngo

Flyngo, the jordanian air carrier and the leading low-cost airline in the Middle East with 45 aircraft, operating more than 1500 weekly flights to 70 domestic and international destinations .Since its launch in 2008,Flyngo has transported more than 60 million passengers.

Recently in 2022, Flyngo has been awarded the Skytrax Award as the Best Low-Cost Airline in the Middle East for the fifth time in a row 2017, 2018, 2019, 2021.

In addition, it was ranked among the top 10 Low-Cost Airline worldwide, as per the prestigious Skytrax which is the most important global forum for the aviation industry.

Flyngo has earned several accolades, including the Middle East's Leading Low-Cost Airline award from the World Travel Awards for eight consecutive years (from 2015 till 2022) as well as it was ranked in the 4-star low-cost carrier category, the highest category of the low-cost airline in the world by APEX rating.
